Austral Interlink International was founded in 2009 by Sukninder Singh with a philosophy distilled to its essence: deliver exceptional results and the clients will follow. Fifteen years of continuous operation have validated that premise.

Our client relationships span generations. We service homes where we cleaned the carpets before the children were born — and now clean those same carpets as the children leave for university. This isn't a transactional business. It's a trusted service embedded in family routines.

From metropolitan Adelaide to the coastal retreats of Victor Harbor and the rural communities of the Yorke Peninsula, we've built a service network that reaches where others don't — and delivers quality that others can't match.

Service Methodology

Walk On Dry™

AII-METHOD-001
PROPRIETARY
01

Immediate Use

Carpets ready to walk on within 1-2 hours, not 24. Essential for families, businesses, and rental turnovers where downtime has direct cost implications.

02

Fibre Safe

Low-moisture technology protects carpet fibres and backing from water damage, mould growth, and shrinkage — extending carpet life and reducing client replacement cycles.

03

Deep Clean

Rotary scrubbing agitates fibres at the base, lifting embedded soil that surface-only methods leave behind. Visible difference. Verifiable results.
