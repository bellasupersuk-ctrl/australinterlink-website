Our Methodology

Walk On Dry™

Our proprietary low-moisture cleaning system delivers superior results with immediate usability — no waiting 24 hours for carpets to dry.

Immediate Use

Carpets are ready to walk on within 1-2 hours, not 24. Perfect for families, businesses, and rental turnovers.

Fibre Safe

Low-moisture technology protects carpet fibres and backing from water damage, mould, and shrinkage.

Deep Clean

Rotary scrubbing agitates fibres at the base, lifting embedded soil that surface-only methods leave behind.
